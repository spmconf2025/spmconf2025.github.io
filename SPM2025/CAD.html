Call for Presentation-only JCAD Papers (TBD)

Presentation-only Papers

We invite authors of Elsevier - Computer-Aided Design (JCAD) papers (excluding evolved papers, survey papers, or communication items) to present their work at the The Symposium on Solid and Physical Modeling 2025 (SPM'25), the flagship conferences of the Solid Modeling Association (SMA).
You will submit your paper as a “CAD Journal Presentation-only” with no further review. By doing this, your research can reach a much broader audience in a timely fashion and receive more feedback.
Accepted submissions will make oral presentations at the conference and be listed in the final program but will not appear in conference proceedings. To be eligible for presentation at SPM'25, your paper must have been published in JCAD between March 1, 2025(TBD), and March 1, 2025(TBD).

Submission guidelines

Please submit only the abstract of the original paper and the complete DOI through the EasyChair website. After logging into the EasyChair system, please select the “SPM2025-CAD Journal Presentation-only Track” for your submission.
